Frequently asked

What counts as an event?

One LLM call, tool invocation, or MCP server response. We bucket per conversation/trace, so you can drill down without paying per span.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes — upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at end of billing cycle. Annual plans get a 20% discount.

How does cost attribution work?

We maintain a curated table of 20+ models (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Meta) with input/output/cached pricing. Each event runs through compute_event_usd() at write time.

What if I exceed my plan's events?

We never drop data. We bill overages at $1 per 100K events on Pro/Team. Free tier rolls back to read-only after the limit.

Can I self-host?

Yes — Enterprise tier ships a Helm chart with Postgres + collector. Contact us for onboarding.

Is my data secure?

TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest. SOC 2 Type II in progress. We never train on your prompts/responses.
