Frequently asked

What's an "event"?

One LLM call, tool invocation or MCP message. Roughly 1:1 with a span in OpenTelemetry.

Do you have a self-hosted option?

Yes — Enterprise tier ships a single Postgres + Rust binary. Run it on your VPC.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your data stays for 30 days after cancellation, then is deleted.

Is there a free trial of Pro?

Free tier already covers most solo use-cases. We don't gate basic FinOps behind a trial.
